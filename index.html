I AM DWAYNE JONES JR.
I GO BY WAYNE.
BORN IN NYC,
LIVING IN NYC, USA.
Hello! I'm Dwayne Jones Jr, a passionate model with roots in the vibrant borough of Brooklyn and the charming city of Raleigh. My journey from the streets of Brooklyn to the runways of New York City has been fueled by a relentless passion for fashion and an unwavering dedication to my craft.

Growing up, I was captivated by the world of fashion and the power of style to transform and express individuality. This fascination led me to pursue modeling as a career, where I could merge my love for fashion with my desire to make a statement. My favorite style is sophistication, a blend of timeless elegance and modern flair that speaks to the heart of who I am.

Now based in the dynamic and fashion-forward city of New York, I am constantly inspired by the energy and diversity around me. Whether I'm walking the runway, posing for editorial shoots, or collaborating with designers, I strive to bring a sense of sophistication and authenticity to every project I undertake.
